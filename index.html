A Knight's Honor

Inspired by classic turn-based RPGs

You, a knight, return back to your village after being sent out on a long mission only to find all the villagers slaughtered by the Forlorn King. He intends to commune with the outer Gods and is on the verge of executing his ritual. You make your way to the Castle grounds only to find the army slaughtered as well, only a handful of the kinds truest remain to carry out his bidding. You must stop him before it's too late.

Instructions:

  1. You always have the first action.
  2. Your character has 20 Health and 10 Stamina. Your Stamina regens to 10 each round.
  3. If your Stamina falls below 0-3 in a round, you take additional damage.
  4. You can choose between: Attack, Heavy-Attack, Block or to Forego a round.
  5. Attacks do 0-10 damage and Heavy-Attacks do 0-15 damage. Yes, you can roll a 0 and miss.
  6. Your block negated 5 damage. But, being defensive constantly has it's own drawbacks.
  7. Reach the Forlorn King before round 30 or risk dooming the World.
